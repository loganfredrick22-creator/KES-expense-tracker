# Dishi — Personal Expense Tracker

**Track every shilling. Own your finances.**

---

## What Problem Does Dishi Solve?

Do you ever wonder where your money went at the end of the month? You had plans to save, but somehow the money disappeared on small purchases you can't even remember. Dishi solves this common problem by helping you:

- **See exactly where your money goes** — Log every expense, no matter how small
- **Stay within budget** — Set a monthly budget and track your spending in real-time
- **Identify spending patterns** — Filter and sort to find your biggest expense categories
- **Make better financial decisions** — With clear data, you can adjust your habits
- **Never lose your data** — Everything saves automatically, even if you close the browser

## Who Is Dishi For?

Dishi is built for anyone who wants to take control of their personal finances:

- **Students** tracking daily expenses on a tight budget
- **Young professionals** building financial awareness
- **Anyone** who wants to stop wondering "where did my money go?"

## What Can You Do With Dishi?

###  Log Your Expenses

Record every purchase with details:
- What you bought (description)
- How much it cost (amount in KSh)
- What category it belongs to (food, transport, housing, etc.)
- When you bought it (date)
- How you paid (M-Pesa, Cash, or Card)

###  Track Your Budget

- Set a monthly budget (default: KSh 30,000)
- Watch a visual progress bar show your spending
- Get color-coded warnings as you approach your limit
- See exactly how much you have left to spend

###  Find Specific Expenses

- Filter by category (show only food expenses)
- Filter by payment method (show only M-Pesa transactions)
- Search descriptions (find "lunch" expenses)
- Sort by date or amount (see your biggest spends first)

###  Export Your Data

Download all your expenses as a CSV file for:
- Spreadsheet analysis in Excel or Google Sheets
- Tax preparation
- Personal record-keeping
- Sharing with a financial advisor

## How to Use Dishi

### Step 1: Add Your First Expense

Fill in the expense form at the top of the page:

- **Description**: What did you spend on? (e.g., "Lunch at Kenchic")
- **Amount**: How much did it cost? (e.g., 350)
- **Category**: Choose from Food, Transport, Housing, Airtime, Health, Entertainment, Education, or Other
- **Date**: When did you spend it? (defaults to today)
- **Payment Method**: M-Pesa, Cash, or Card

Click "Add Expense" to save it.

### Step 2: Set Your Monthly Budget

The default budget is KSh 30,000. To change it:

1. Find the "Monthly Budget" section
2. Edit the amount in the input field
3. Your budget saves automatically

The progress bar will show how much of your budget you've used:
-  **Green**: You're under 70% of your budget — you're doing great!
   **Yellow**: You're at 70-90% — watch your spending
   **Red**: You're over 90% — time to slow down

### Step 3: Review Your Spending

The dashboard at the top shows:
- **Total Spent**: How much you've spent this month
- **Budget Remaining**: How much you have left
- **Expenses This Month**: How many transactions you've made
- **Top Category**: Where you spend the most money

### Step 4: Find Specific Expenses

Use the filters to find exactly what you're looking for:

- **Filter by Category**: See only food expenses, only transport, etc.
- **Filter by Payment**: Show only M-Pesa transactions, only cash, etc.
- **Search**: Type words to find expenses (e.g., "lunch" finds all lunch expenses)
- **Sort**: Order by newest, oldest, highest amount, or lowest amount

All filters work together — you can combine them to find exactly what you need.

### Step 5: Export Your Data

Click "Export to CSV" to download all your expenses as a spreadsheet file. You can open this in Excel, Google Sheets, or any spreadsheet program.

## Tips for Getting the Most Out of Dishi

- **Log everything** — Even small purchases add up. Log that KSh 50 matatu ride.
- **Be consistent** — Try to log expenses daily so you don't forget
- **Review weekly** — Check your dashboard once a week to spot patterns
- **Adjust your budget** — If you consistently overspend in one category, adjust your habits

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Is my data private?

Yes. All your data is stored in your browser's local storage. Nothing is sent to any server. Your financial information never leaves your device.

### What if I clear my browser data?

Clearing your browser data will delete your expenses. To avoid this:
- Export your data regularly using the CSV export feature
- Don't clear "Site Data" or "Local Storage" when clearing your browser cache

### Can I use Dishi on my phone?

Yes! Dishi is designed to work perfectly on mobile devices. The layout adjusts automatically to your screen size.

### What currency does Dishi use?

Dishi uses Kenya Shilling (KSh) by default, but it can be adapted for any currency. See the technical documentation for developers if you want to customize it.

### Is Dishi free?

Yes, Dishi is completely free. No subscriptions, no ads, no hidden costs.

### Can I sync my data across devices?

Currently, Dishi stores data locally on each device. Cross-device sync is planned for a future version.
